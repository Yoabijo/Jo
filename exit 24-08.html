1. Which type of shallow foundation consists of a continuous strip of concrete around the perimeter of the building?

Answer: A. Strip Foundation. Explanation: A strip foundation is a type of shallow foundation that consists of a continuous strip of concrete around the perimeter of the building.

2. What is the purpose of a pile cap in pile foundation construction?

Answer: A. To distribute the load from the column over the piles. Explanation: A pile cap is used to distribute the load from the column over the piles in a pile foundation.

3. Why might a combined pier be used instead of a tension or compression pier?

Answer: C. To accommodate changing soil conditions. Explanation: A combined pier may be used to accommodate changing soil conditions, which can affect the load-bearing capacity of the foundation.

4. Which type of excavation technique is typically used for deep foundation construction?

Answer: B. Mechanical excavation. Explanation: Mechanical excavation is typically used for deep excavation in foundation construction due to the depth and scale of the excavation.

5. Which type of foundation is typically used for a heavy industrial building?

Answer: B. Deep foundation. Explanation: Heavy industrial buildings typically require deep foundation due to the weight and load requirements of the building.

6. What is the minimum depth of excavation for a deep foundation?

Answer: B. 2 meters. Explanation: The minimum depth of excavation for a deep foundation is usually around 2 meters.

7. Which of the following is an example of a shallow foundation?

Answer: C. Raft foundation. Explanation: Raft foundation is an example of a shallow foundation.

8. Which type of foundation is commonly used for building on sloping terrain?

Answer: C. Stepped foundation. Explanation: A stepped foundation may be used for building on sloping terrain, which can help to create a level surface for the foundation.

9. How is the bearing capacity of soil determined for foundation construction?

Answer: C. By conducting a soil test. Explanation: The bearing capacity of soil is determined by conducting a soil test, which helps to determine the loadbearing capacity of the soil.

10. Which type of foundation is commonly used for residential buildings?

Answer: A. Shallow foundation. Explanation: Shallow foundations are commonly used for residential buildings due to their cost-effectiveness and ease of construction.
