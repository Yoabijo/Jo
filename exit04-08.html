DAY 4,1 EXIT Questions

1. Which of the following is a flooring system that is known for its sustainability and durability?

Answer: D. Hardwood flooring. Hardwood flooring is a sustainable and durable flooring system that is made from natural materials and can last for decades with the proper maintenance.

2. Which of the following is an important principle of cost-efficient construction?

Answer: C. Optimizing material usage. Cost-efficient construction involves finding ways to reduce waste and optimize material usage while maintaining the quality and safety of the building.

3. Which of the following is a common water-resistant material used in exterior building walls?

Answer: D. Vinyl siding. Vinyl siding is a water-resistant material that is commonly used in exterior building walls due to its low cost and durability.

4. Which of the following structural concepts is used to distribute the weight of a building evenly across its foundation?

Answer: A. Load bearing. Load bearing is a structural concept used to distribute the weight of a building evenly across its foundation, utilizing the strength and stability of the materials used.

5. Which of the following is a building system that allows for the efficient use of energy in buildings?

Answer: C. Passive solar system. Passive solar systems are designed to utilize the energy from the sun to heat and cool buildings in an efficient and sustainable way.

6. Which of the following is a material used for insulation in buildings?

Answer: C. Fiberglass. Fiberglass is a common insulation material used in buildings that helps to regulate temperature, reduce energy consumption, and improve overall comfort.

7. Which of the following is an advantage of using masonry construction?

Answer: C. High resistance to natural disasters. Masonry construction is known for its strength, durability, and resistance to natural disasters such as earthquakes, hurricanes, and fires.

8. Which of the following is a common flooring system used in commercial and industrial buildings?

Answer: a. Vinyl tile. Vinyl tile is a popular and durable flooring system used in commercial and industrial buildings due to its ability to withstand heavy traffic and moisture.

9. Which of the following is a type of concrete used in building construction?

Answer: B. Reinforced concrete. Reinforced concrete is a type of concrete used in building construction that is reinforced with steel to increase its strength and durability.

10. Which of the following is a type of wall system that is easy to install and has good sound insulation properties?

Answer: C. Steel stud walls. Steel stud walls are a type of wall system that is easy to install and has good sound insulation properties due to the presence of an air gap between the studs.
